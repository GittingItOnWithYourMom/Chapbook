You must not fear the hard path You must not fear it’s fearful wrath Although the hard path may seem unforgiving At the end you will find all the giving When the fruits of our labor are rewarded Those on the easy path will not be awarded But the few who took all the risk Will stand on top an obelisk But if you think the pain is not worth the ending Think again, for it’s only about attending Because even when you might be screaming It's overcoming the fear that gives us meaning And once you reach the end of the road You’ll look back proud and old So when you must pick between two paths You must travel the hard path
